Dequan Xiao, PhD
Jacob F. Buckman Chair and Associate Professor
  • Chemistry Graduate Program Coordinator, 2018-present
  • Campus Director, NASA CT Space Grant Consortium, 2014-2019
  • University Research Scholar, 2016-2019

Dequan Xiao is the director of Center for Integrative Materials Discovery and Endowed Jacob F. Buckman Chair and Associate Professor at the University of New Haven (UNH). He joined the faculty of UNH in August 2013. He teaches the courses of Physical Chemistry and Laboratory, and Polymer Science. He is the coordinator for Physical Chemistry Laboratory.
